Typography Scale

Font Family: Inter (Google Fonts) — Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900

Hero Title

H1 / Hero 64px (4rem) Weight: 900 (Black) Line-height: 1.1 Use: Hero headlines

Section Title

H2 / Section 48px (3rem) Weight: 800 (ExtraBold) Line-height: 1.2 Use: Section headings

Large Title

H3 / Large 40px (2.5rem) Weight: 800 (ExtraBold) Line-height: 1.2 Use: Solution titles

Card Title

H4 / Card 24px (1.5rem) Weight: 700 (Bold) Line-height: 1.3 Use: Card headings, feature titles

Body text large for hero descriptions and important paragraphs that need more presence.

Body Large 20px (1.25rem) Weight: 400 (Regular) Line-height: 1.7 Use: Hero descriptions

Body text is the standard paragraph style used throughout the site for descriptions and content.

Body 16px (1rem) Weight: 400 (Regular) Line-height: 1.6 Use: General content
Section Tag
Label / Tag 14px (0.9rem) Weight: 700 (Bold) Letter-spacing: 2px Use: Section labels
Small text for captions, metadata, and supporting information.
Small 13.6px (0.85rem) Weight: 500 (Medium) Line-height: 1.5 Use: Captions, metadata
